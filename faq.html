Help

Welcome to the help page of SHOPFYX. Here you can find answers to common questions and get assistance with using our website.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How can I place an order?

To place an order, follow these steps:

  1. Browse our website and add the desired items to your cart.
  2. Proceed to checkout and provide your shipping and payment information.
  3. Review your order summary and confirm your purchase.

2. How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status of your delivery on our website or the courier's website.

3. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. You can choose your preferred payment option during checkout.

4. How can I contact customer support?

If you need assistance or have any inquiries, you can contact our customer support team via email at support@shopfyx.com or call us at +1-800-123-4567. Our team is available to assist you from Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (EST).
